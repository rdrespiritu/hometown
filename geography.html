GEOGRAPHY

Vigan is a city in the northern part of Luzon, one of the three main Philippine islands. It is the capital of Ilocos Sur since before the Spanish conquerors arrived in the Philippines and has long been a significant political and commercial center. It still maintains its status as a very accessible hub because of its excellent geographic features and location. Situated 408 kilometers from Manila, around 80 kilometers between it and Laoag City, Ilocos Norte, a stunning view of the South China Sea is in full display providing visitors with picture-perfect view of the sea that greets its visitors with the pleasant coastal breeze. The nearby green rice fields and agricultural landscapes also give a glimpse of the countryside living, making it a perfect choice for visitors wanting to enjoy the laid-back feel of the province life.
