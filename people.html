PEOPLE

Much like most of the Filipinos, the people of Vigan are very hospitable and friendly, making visitors feel at home as they visit the city. Their strong sense of community is a clear display of their rich cultural heritage which they take much pride in.

According to the last recorded census as of 2020, Vigan has a population of a total of 53,935 which represents 7.64% of the total population of the Province of Ilocos Sur.

Known for their warm and welcoming nature, the “Viganeños” as they are often called, are deeply passionate about preserving their rich cultural legacy and proudly share it with the visitors. They are also known for their culinary expertise making the city known for its delectable Ilocano cuisine such as the famous Empanada, Bagnet, Vigan Longganisa among others. In general, Vigan residents reflect the city's vibrant culture and dedication to maintaining their distinctive way of life.
