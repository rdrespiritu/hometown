Historic City of Vigan

Welcome to the charming city of Vigan, where rich cultural traditions abound and history is whispered through the cobblestone streets. Situated in the center of the Philippines, Vigan is a UNESCO World Heritage Site that welcomes you to travel back in time and immerse yourself in its rich historical legacy.

Known for its well-preserved Spanish colonial architecture, Vigan is a living testament to the country's vibrant heritage. The fusion of Asian and European influences is evident in every corner, from the iconic Calle Crisologo to the majestic St. Paul's Metropolitan Cathedral.

Calle Crisologo, Vigan City, Philippines

Burnay Pottery - Vigan City, Philippines

Experience the city's distinct combination of customs as you stroll around, from the exquisite flavors of the local cuisine to the meticulous weaving of Abel Iloco fabrics. Experience the welcoming warmth of Vigan as you go through historical sites, museums, and bustling marketplaces.

For those who have an interest in history, cuisine, or the allure of a bygone age, Vigan offers a really remarkable experience. Experience the enchantment of this UNESCO treasure, where history comes to life and every visit offers a window into the essence of Philippine culture and history. Welcome to Vigan, a place where history is felt in the present.
